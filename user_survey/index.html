4th EasyBuild User Survey (2020)

For the last 4 years, we have conducted a user survey in the EasyBuild community in an attempt to collect (anonymous) feedback, and to get better insight into the community.

The most recent survey was conducted Dec'20 - Jan'21.
The raw survey results are available here (PDF).

The survey results are presented during the annual "State of the Union" talk at the EasyBuild User Meeting:

  • 2019 (3rd user survey): slides (PDF) - recorded talk (YouTube)
  • 2018 (2nd user survey): slides (PDF) - recorded talk (YouTube)
  • 2017 (1st user survey): slides (PDF) - recorded talk (YouTube)
